About SofaSoGood

Transforming houses into homes with quality furniture and thoughtful design since 2019

Our Story

SofaSoGood began in 2019 with a simple mission: to make beautiful, quality furniture accessible to everyone in Cambodia. What started as a small showroom in Phnom Penh has grown into a beloved brand that helps families create their dream homes.

We believe that everyone deserves a space they love coming home to. That's why we carefully curate every piece in our collection, ensuring it meets our high standards for quality, comfort, and style.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of happy customers across Cambodia, offering everything from cozy sofas to elegant decorations that make a house feel like home.
